Mission

The mission of Hermanos Macehual Alumni Association is dedicated to fostering a network of united HM Alumni who in the spirit and traditions of Hermanos Macehual will provide professional fellowship amongst alumni and engage in community service to underserved communities in any areas of need: including education, health, cultural awareness, and social justice. In providing these services we will keep in mind that the creation of our organization was firmly rooted in Latino/Indigenous culture and our actions should, in perpetuity, always have an impact rooted in our origins.

Our organization is committed to establishing a network of ideas, actions and united professionals nationwide that will support the values of the Hermanos Macehual Alumni Association.

Our organization is committed to establishing a national network of professionals that will support the values and actions of the Hermanos Macehual Alumni Association.
